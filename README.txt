This code is for the paper

Lei Zhang, Meng Yang, and Xiangchu Feng,
"Sparse Representation or Collaborative Representation: Which Helps Face Recognition?", in ICCV 2011.

The AR and AR occlusion datasets used in our paper can be downloaded at:
http://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~csmyang/database_mat/AR_database.mat
http://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~csmyang/database_mat/AR_database_Occlusion.mat

For the experiments on Gender classification, Extended YaleB and MPIE datasets, please refer to the experimental settings described in our paper.

For any problems with the code, please contact us at csmyang@comp.polyu.edu.hk or cslzhang@comp.polyu.edu.hk.
